============================================================

The current local inference script creates the ERA5 input as:

field = np.zeros((1, 1, 10, 33, 33), dtype="float32")

Therefore local inference does not use a real current ERA5 atmospheric field.

The model was trained on real ERA5 fields but is evaluated locally using a zero-filled field. This creates a major train/inference mismatch. The atmospheric branch therefore receives little or no live environmental information, and the forecast is driven mostly by the short historical track.

- Weak target definitions

- Poor treatment of intensity and storm size
