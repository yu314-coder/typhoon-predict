Environmental gap closed. SHIPS alone covered 7% of WP+EP test windows (it ends 2021). Adding AOML ocean heat brings the merged set to 82%. The two cross-check: on 8,053 shared windows their ocean-heat values correlate at r = 0.798 — independent pipelines, different match keys, same answer.
ERA5 is downloading, newest-first, slowly. RDA's OPeNDAP endpoint went through a spell of ~50% request failures; it has since recovered. 2024–2026 are down (2 clean full years + the partial 2026 that ends the archive), 2,296 WP windows so far. The remaining test years (2020–2023) are landing at roughly one every 1–2 h. This is the 10×-resolution vortex patch v24's failure said we need — it layers onto v25 later without redoing anything.

Ocean heat starts 2013: AOML's advertised 2012 is entirely NaN over our box. Basin drops to 40% in 2026 and ERA5 stops after March 2026 — the reanalyses simply do not extend to the late-2026 storms yet.

47,556 WP+EP windows still have no environmental features — almost all pre-2013, before either ocean source begins. v25 consumes the coverage mask as an input rather than assuming features exist.
